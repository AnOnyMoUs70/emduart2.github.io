Seminare - IAG

The goal of this seminar is to learn about two tools from symbolic algebra to solve systems of polynomial equations. The first one is the theory of Grobner bases and the second one is the theory of resultants. We will discuss theorems and implementations of these tools in a computer algebra system. The main two references for the seminar are the books
  • 1. Using Algebraic Geometry(UAG) by Cox, Little and Oshea, and
  • 2. Ideals, Varieties and Algorithms(IVA) by Cox, Little and Oshea.
